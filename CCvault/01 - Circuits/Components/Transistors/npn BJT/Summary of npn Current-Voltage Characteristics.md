1. $$V_{BE}\gt V_{BEon}\approx 0.5$$ typically $V_{BE}=0.7$

3. $$I_{C}=I_{S}\exp\left( \frac{V_{BE}}{V_{T}} \right)$$
4. $$I_{C}=\beta I_{B}$$
