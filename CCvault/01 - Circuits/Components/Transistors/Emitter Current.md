$$I_{E}=I_{C}+I_{B}$$
Using the [[Base Current]] equation 

$$I_{E}=I_{C}+\dfrac{I_{C}}{\beta}$$
$$I_{C}=\alpha I_{E}$$
where
$$\alpha=\dfrac{\beta}{\beta+1}$$
$\alpha=1$ when $\beta$ is large
