$$SNR=\frac{P_{R}}{P_{\tilde{n}}}=\frac{P_{R}}{wN_{o}}$$
Where
$\large P_{\tilde{n}}=$ [[Power of Filtered Noise]]
$P_{R}=$ [[Received Signal Power]]

# SNR in Decibels

dB:
$$10\log(SNR)=SNR$$
$SNR=1\implies 10\log(1)=0$
$SNR=10\implies 10\log(10)=10$
$SNR=100\implies 10\log(100)=20$
